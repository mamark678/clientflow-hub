## Short Description (132 chars max)
Manage multiple client workspaces without the chaos. Switch instantly between client accounts and tools with one click.

## Detailed Description

ClientFlow is the ultimate browser extension for freelancers, agencies, and consultants who manage multiple clients. Say goodbye to the chaos of juggling dozens of tabs, logging in and out of accounts, and losing track of project links.

With ClientFlow, you can create dedicated workspaces for each client, complete with their color codes, specific tool URLs, and even auto-detected Google Account configurations.

### üöÄ Key Features

*   **Organized Client Workspaces**: Create a dedicated card for each client with their name and custom color coding.
*   **One-Click Tool Access**: Store all important links for a client (Trello, Drive, Slack, website, etc.) and open them instantly.
*   **Google Account Indexing**: Stop fighting with "You don't have access" errors! ClientFlow automatically detects and saves the correct Google account index (u/0, u/1, etc.) for each client's Drive and Gmail links.
*   **Cloud Sync**: Sign in to sync your client workspaces across all your devices (optional).
*   **Privacy First**: Use it locally without an account, or sync securely via Supabase. Your data is yours.
